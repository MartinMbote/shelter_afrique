ARRIVAL AT THE AIRPORT

Almost all participants will arrive at the Houari Boumediene International Airport (ALG), located around 45 kilometres from Algiers city centre. The drive from
the central business district to the airport takes around 25 to 30 minutes. A transport service organised by the Ministry will be set up to welcome official delegations
to the ALG. This service will transfer delegates to their respective hotels. Individual vehicles will be made available to Ministers and Heads of Delegation. There are
also organised and registered airport taxis that can take you from the airport to the city centre or your hotel.

The taxi fare to the city centre varies from 3,000 to 5,000 DZD (three thousand to five thousand Algerian dinars, or around USD 15 to 25), depending on your destination. However, for your first visit, it is recommended that you use the transport service organised by the Ministry to ensure a safe and comfortable welcome. It is advisable to refer to official sources and local information for specific, up-todate advice on the details of transport from the airport in People’s Democratic Republic of Algeria.

VISA REQUIREMENTS (INCLUDING THE COST OF VISAS FOR
COUNTRIES THAT REQUIRE THEM)

It is the responsibility of each delegate to obtain a visa for People’s Democratic Republic of Algeria. Please contact the nearest Algerian embassy or consulate for
further information. In exceptional cases, the host country will plan to issue entry visas locally for those who are unable to obtain one prior to arrival. Please note
that you will need to pay your visa fee on arrival at the airport.

Arrangements have been made with Algerian embassies around the world to facilitate visa requests for the Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM). It is advisable to
refer to official sources and local information for specific, up-to-date details of the procedures for obtaining visas for AGEX in People’s Democratic Republic of Algeria.
